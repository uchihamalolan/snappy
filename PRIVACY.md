# Privacy Policy for Snap Screen

Last Updated: July 20, 2026

Snap Screen ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension, Snap Screen (the "Extension").

By installing and using the Extension, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information Collection and Usage

Snap Screen is designed to process all user data locally on your device. We do not operate external servers, and we do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, media, or browsing history.

Here is how we use Chrome extension permissions to provide features:
- **Storage**: We use the `storage` permission to store your local configuration preferences (e.g., active capture mode, destination path, and active recording state) on your own device.
- **Active Tab & Tab Capture**: We use the `activeTab` and `tabCapture` permissions to access the video and audio stream of the active tab when you explicitly trigger the Extension. This stream is processed locally inside your browser to generate your screenshots and video recordings.
- **Scripting**: We use the `scripting` permission to inspect page dimensions of `<video>` elements to crop screenshots accurately.
- **Offscreen**: We use the `offscreen` API to process WebRTC streams locally on your device to export media.

---

## 2. No Data Sharing and Third-Party Transfer

- **No Remote Processing**: All video recordings and screenshots are processed locally in your browser.
- **Zero Third-Party Transfers**: We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to any third parties, including advertising platforms, data brokers, or information resellers.

---

## 3. Google API Limited Use Policy Compliance

The use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements:
1. We only use access to Google APIs to provide or improve the core user-facing features of the Extension.
2. We do not transfer this data to third parties, unless necessary to provide or improve the Extension's single purpose, to comply with applicable laws, or as part of a security investigation.
3. We do not allow humans to read user data.
4. We do not use or transfer user data for personalized, interest-based, or behavioral advertising.
